Census Data

Using Python's requests library, we make calls to the US Census' API to obtain data for counties in all 50 states. We gathered data for each county including population, total number of houses and median family income.

Who Are We.

This is a CapStone project comprised of 4 team members 2 working on the front end, and 2 working on the back end. We a strong group of students from Fayetteville Technical Community College who are taking the past two years to produce a product from all that we have learned. We have learned in order for a project to be successful the team must work as a family, use each others strengths while building each others weakness up.

Our Process.

With the scale of the project both teams set off on each section for the first 12 weeks. In week 14 we joined back as one team to work on combining the two projects into one. The Front-End team split each section of the site into bit sized bits that each could work one. The Back-End team followed a similar suite but need to work less independently as the Front-End team.

Our Approach.

We set a deadline for each part of the project to have a usable part so the two projects and be combined into one. With our group being split into two each group sets its members roles and work time frame. For the Front-End Jesse handles most of the presentable's and sub pages for the site. Brian is tasked with the overall design of the site and handling the merger of both projects.

Our Goal.

GLAPS over all goal is the ability to take a current home value and by using the US Census be able to calculate the value of the home up x years.

Our Mission.

Provide the best product from what we have learned while over stepping our own abilities to learn material outside of our course work.

Fayetteville Technical Community College (FTCC or, informally, Fay Tech) is a community college in Fayetteville, North Carolina. The institution is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges (SACSCOC) and is a member of the North Carolina Community College System. FTCC serves more than 41,000 students annually by providing over 190 occupational, technical, general education, college transfer, and continuing education programs.
